    Welcome to the Hindustan National Scholarship Competition (HNSC)! Our mission is to empower talented students and provide them with the opportunity to soar to new heights. Every year our scholorship provide many talented students opportunity to enhance there learning ability,improvment in studies,etc. This year we our conducting ALL OVER INDIA to select the talented one and give them some support for there future.



      Why HNSC?
      At HNSC, we believe that every student possesses unique abilities waiting to be discovered.
      Our competition aims to recognize and nurture these talents. By conducting HNSC, we hope to:
    • Unleash Potential: We want to unlock the hidden potential within students by challenging them intellectually and creatively.
    • Equal Opportunity: HNSC is open to students from standard 8th to 12th, ensuring that everyone has an equal chance to participate.
    • Scholarship Rewards: Outstanding performers will receive scholarships to support their educational journey.


      How to Apply?
    • Applying for HNSC is straightforward! Students in grades 8 to 12 can follow these steps:
    • Registration: Visit our official website and complete the registration form.
    • Eligibility: Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria (based on your grade level).
    • Subject Selection: Choose from the following four subjects for the exam: English, Maths, Science, and Geography.
      Prepare: Study hard and prepare well for the competition.
    Exam Date: Keep an eye out for the exam date announcement.

      Exam Subjects
      The HNSC exam covers the following subjects:
    • English: Test your language skills, comprehension, and writing abilities.
    • Maths: Challenge your mathematical reasoning and problem-solving skills.
    • Science: Explore scientific concepts and critical thinking.
    • Geography: Dive into the world of maps, landscapes, and global awareness.
